Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. PWA Counter is designed to be a simple, private tool for tracking counts. Here is a compact overview of how we handle your data.

1. Data We Collect

We only collect the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Identity: When you log in via Google OAuth, we store your email and name to identify your account.
  • User Content: We store the counters you create and the counts you record.
  • Session Data: We use a temporary session cookie to keep you logged in.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to operate the PWA Counter service. We do not:

  • Sell your data to third parties.
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing.
  • Track your activity outside of this application.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored securely in a private database. Access is restricted to the application's backend service. We do not share your data with any external services or third-party providers.

4. Your Rights & Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time via the Account settings. This action anonymizes your user record and clears your session.
  • Access: You can view and manage your counters directly within the app.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our service. Any significant changes will be noted on this page.
